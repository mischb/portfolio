About me

I'm a fullstack developer, with a passion for well-crafted, logical code. Before becoming a programmer, I worked as a teacher-educator, developing accessible math curriculum for early years students. Programing is, for me, a tool for bettering the world, by connecting people, and automating the mundane in order to free up time and energy for people to follow their passions. I'm excited about all things Javascript. From node.js, to react.js, I love to build beautiful, mobile-ready, applications. With an eye to social justice, and to education - in the broadest sense - I am following my dreams to do what I love, and to make the world a better place. If you love programming, problem-solving, and puzzles, I'd love to chat! Contact me by email or find me on github. Thanks for visiting!
